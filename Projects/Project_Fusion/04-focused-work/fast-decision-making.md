# 👩‍🏫 Fast Decision Making

**Commercial Value:**
Decision-making is the core of almost everything. Being good at it is one of the biggest leverages for the company and for you personally.

**Problem:**
- **Speed:** 18.5% of an employee's total time is literally wasted due to ineffective decision-making (37% of time is spent on decisions overall).
- **Quality:** Repeatedly making wrong decisions leads to a bad product and a bad company.

---

## Principles

- Decide based on reversibility and potential impact.
- Seek dissenting opinions — get challenged.
- Start with an opinion on what the decision will be, not with facts.

**Additional notes:**
- Do not aim to be 100% right.
- Intuition is great in areas where you have deep experience. If you have no experience in something, don't trust your intuition.
- Be aware of the assumptions behind your decisions.

---

## Decision Matrix

| | **High Impact** | **Low Impact** |
|---|---|---|
| **Irreversible** | Heavy investment — find ways to test reversible parts first | Copy proven approaches |
| **Reversible** | Low initial investment, high investment in feedback and learning | Delegate, empower your team, or make a quick call |

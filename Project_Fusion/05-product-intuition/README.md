# 🧠 Product Intuition

Product intuition refers to the instinct great PMs develop to make great product decisions, even in the absence of detailed information.

You develop product intuition by constantly exposing yourself to what customers are saying. Over thousands of data points, you start to instinctively understand what will work and what won't.

**Underlying issue:** Limited exposure to customers because focus is on daily operations.

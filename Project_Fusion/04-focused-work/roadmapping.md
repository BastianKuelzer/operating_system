# 🗺️ Roadmapping

We have endless opportunities with our product and will likely drown in chasing ideas rather than running out of them. To prevent this, we take bets on features and ideas to work on for the next 6 weeks and fully commit.

**What is a bet?**
A bet is a written document about the problem and a rough solution sketch — detailed enough that engineering and design can work autonomously, but not so detailed that they become ticket takers.

---

## Process Overview

- **Discovery:** Shape potential bets (parallel to the current cycle)
- **Betting Table:** Prioritize and commit to bets for the next cycle
- **Delivery:** Execute the selected bets within a 6-week window
- **Cool Down:** 1 week between delivery cycles to decide on bets and fix smaller issues

---

## Aligning long-term vision with the next feature to build

- The 6-week bets give aligned focus on what will move the company forward in the next 6 weeks. After deciding, there is full focus on execution.
- Part of the 6-week bets includes strategy discussions and iterations on where the company should head. You can include hypothesis-driven bets to prove or disprove them — this ensures enough capacity is allocated to verify key assumptions.
